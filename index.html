About Our Home Daycare

Nora's Daycare is run by May Jubran, a licensed childcare provider with more than 13 years of experience. After years working in a Montessori preschool, May has crafted an in‑home program that nurtures each child's curiosity and wonder. A low caregiver‑to‑child ratio ensures every child receives individual attention in a safe, home‑like setting.

Family partnership is at the heart of our philosophy: we encourage parents to get involved, share cultural traditions and communicate openly through our open‑door policy. Our curriculum blends play‑based learning, music, art, outdoor exploration and, of course, plenty of hugs!

Our Program

A typical day at Nora's Daycare.

Structured Learning

We introduce letters, numbers, colors and shapes through games, songs and hands‑on activities that encourage curiosity.

Creative Play

Art projects, imaginative play and dress‑up help children express themselves and build social skills.

Outdoor Fun

We spend time outside each day exploring our fenced yard, tending to our garden and enjoying fresh air.

Daily Schedule & Hours

A structured yet flexible routine keeps children engaged and happy.

  • 6:30 AM – 9:00 AM: Drop‑off & free play

  • 11:30 AM – 12:30 PM: Lunch & story time
  • 12:30 PM – 2:30 PM: Nap/quiet time
  • 2:30 PM – 3:30 PM: Music & movement
  • 3:30 PM – 6:00 PM: Snack & pick‑up

Our daycare operates Monday‑Friday, 6:30 AM – 6:00 PM. Extended hours may be available upon request.
